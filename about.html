About the Author

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm Maya Johnson, a personal empowerment coach, author, and founder of Empowered Living. I'm passionate about helping individuals discover their authentic voice and create meaningful change in their lives.

My Story

For most of my early life, I thought success meant following the rules, meeting external expectations, and staying safely within established boundaries. I excelled academically, built a promising career in corporate marketing, and checked all the boxes of what society deemed successful.

But beneath the surface achievements, I felt increasingly disconnected from my authentic self. I had become skilled at being who others needed me to be, while losing touch with my own desires, boundaries, and voice.

My wake-up call came after a burnout that left me physically ill and emotionally depleted. In that painful space, I realized I had been living according to everyone else's definition of success except my own.

"True empowerment isn't about having power over others, but about reclaiming power over your own choices, boundaries, and direction. It's the quiet revolution of choosing yourself."

That realization began a journey of rediscovery that transformed my relationship with myself and eventually led me to my true calling: helping others navigate their own path to authentic empowerment.

Today, I blend evidence-based psychology, mindfulness practices, and practical tools to support individuals who are ready to move from external validation to internal authority. Whether through my writing, workshops, or one-on-one coaching, my mission is to create spaces where people can reconnect with their innate wisdom and power.
